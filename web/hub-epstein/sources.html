Source Verification Standards

All sources must meet our verification requirements:

✅ Authenticity

Documents verified through official channels and cross-referenced with original sources

🔓 Public Availability

Only publicly accessible documents are included; no sealed or restricted materials

📋 Documentation

Every piece of information includes clear source citations and links when available

🔍 Multi-Source Verification

Critical information verified through multiple independent sources

Exclusions

We do NOT use the following sources:

  • Anonymous submissions without verification
  • Unverified social media claims
  • Conspiracy theory websites
  • Documents from unreliable or biased sources
  • Sealed court documents or classified materials
  • Information that violates victim privacy
