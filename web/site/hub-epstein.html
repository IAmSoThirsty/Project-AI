Hub of Epstein Files Directory

A comprehensive, neutral organizational hub that aggregates and links to publicly available files and documentation related to the Epstein matter — provided exclusively as a research and educational resource for journalists, scholars, and civic observers who need a single, well-indexed entry point into already-public material.

⚠ Disclaimer · Read Before Proceeding

This project does not publish original allegations, conclusions, or any non-public information. It is a directory of links to records that are already in the public domain through court filings, government releases, and reputable journalism. The author makes no claims about any individual mentioned in those records and takes no editorial position. The presence of any name, document, or link is not a statement of guilt, association, or fact. Use this resource responsibly, with appropriate journalistic and legal standards. If you are a subject of any linked record and believe a link is in error, please contact via the contact page.

01scope

Index, not archive

The directory points to files hosted by their original publishers — courts, government agencies, news outlets — never re-hosting copyrighted or sensitive originals.

02method

Automated workflows

Ingestion is governed by automated tagging and provenance pipelines. Every link includes a source-of-record, retrieval timestamp, and content hash where applicable.

03use

For researchers

Designed for journalists, academic researchers, civic technologists, and policy analysts who need one well-organized starting point into an otherwise scattered public record.

Editorial principles

What this project will not do.

  • Publish original allegations or unverified claims about any individual.
  • Re-host content that infringes copyright or that was not lawfully released.
  • Aggregate content for any purpose other than research and education.
  • Provide an editorial opinion on any person, allegation, or proceeding.
  • Accept submissions of non-public material.
