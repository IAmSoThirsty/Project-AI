About Epstein Files Hub

A comprehensive archive and organized presentation of publicly available information

Our Mission

The Epstein Files Hub serves as a comprehensive digital archive and research portal for publicly available documents, court filings, and verified information related to the Jeffrey Epstein case. Our mission is to:

  • Provide organized access to public documents and information
  • Enable researchers, journalists, and the public to discover connections and patterns
  • Maintain the highest standards of accuracy and verification
  • Respect victim privacy and dignity in all presentations
  • Support transparency and accountability through public information

Our Technology

The Epstein Files Hub is built on a modern, open-source technology stack designed for transparency, performance, and accessibility:

GitHub Pages - Free, reliable hosting with 99.9%+ uptime
Client-Side Search - Fast, private search using Lunr.js with no server tracking
Git LFS - Large file support for PDFs, images, and videos
GitHub Actions - Automated workflows for continuous updates
Responsive Design - Mobile-first approach for all devices
Cloudflare CDN - Global content delivery with SSL/TLS encryption

Data Sources

All information in the Epstein Files Hub is sourced from public, verified sources:

  • Court Documents: SDNY, SDFL, and other federal court filings
  • Government Records: FBI releases, DOJ announcements, FAA records
  • Media Archives: DocumentCloud, Internet Archive, Wikimedia Commons
  • Wikipedia Integration: Weekly data updates from verified Wikipedia articles
  • Uncensored.ai Database: Hourly extraction from public Epstein files database
  • Official Sources: Justice.gov, FBI.gov, and other government websites

Privacy & Ethics

Our Commitment

We are committed to responsible presentation of sensitive information:

  • Victim Privacy: We prioritize victim privacy and follow court-ordered redactions
  • Public Records Only: Information is included only if it appears in public court documents
  • No Speculation: We present facts from verified sources without editorial commentary
  • Respectful Presentation: Content is presented with sensitivity to the serious nature of the case
  • Transparency: All sources are cited and verifiable

Who We Are

The Epstein Files Hub is maintained by a combination of human oversight and AI agents:

  • 26 GitHub Copilot Agents - Specialized agents for data integration, processing, analysis, and maintenance
  • 11 Python Bot Systems - Document processing, search indexing, and quality control
  • Human Oversight - All automated content is reviewed and verified by humans
  • Open Source - The entire codebase is open source on GitHub for transparency
