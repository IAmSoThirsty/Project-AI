Methodology & Data Integrity

All visualizations are based on verified public records. Our process:

  1. Source Verification: All data comes from court documents, government records, or verified sources

  4. Regular Updates: Visualizations are updated as new documents are released
  5. Peer Review: Complex visualizations undergo community review
