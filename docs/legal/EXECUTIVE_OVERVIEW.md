## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Project-AI delivers a production-grade, open-source AI assistant platform with enterprise governance, cryptographic security, and ethical decision-making frameworks. Unlike proprietary "black box" AI solutions, Project-AI provides full transparency, self-hosting capabilities, and court-defensible audit trails.

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Open Source (MIT License):** Full code ownership and auditability
- **Ethical AI:** Built-in Asimov's Laws framework prevents harmful actions
- **Zero Vendor Lock-In:** Self-host or use managed services
- **Cryptographic Governance:** Immutable audit trails and acceptance ledgers
- **Tiered Affordability:** Free for individuals, affordable for organizations

## GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE

### Legal Framework

**Acceptance Ledger:** Cryptographically-secured, immutable record of all agreements
- SHA-256 hash chaining for tamper-evidence
- Ed25519 digital signatures for authentication
- Hardware-backed signing (TPM/HSM) for enterprise/government
- RFC 3161 timestamp authority for legal timestamping

**Jurisdictional Compliance:** Modular annexes for global regulatory requirements
- GDPR (European Union)
- CCPA (California)
- PIPEDA (Canada)
- UK DPA 2018 (United Kingdom)
- Australia Privacy Act

**Enforcement:** Boot-time and runtime validation of acceptance and entitlements

### Corporate Governance

**Decision Authority:**
- Strategic Direction: Board of Directors (if incorporated) / Community (if foundation)
- Product Roadmap: Technical Steering Committee
- Security Policy: Security Council
- Compliance: Compliance Officer + Legal Counsel

**Transparency:**
- Public acceptance ledger verification endpoint
- Open source codebase (GitHub)
- Public roadmap and issue tracking
- Quarterly transparency reports
