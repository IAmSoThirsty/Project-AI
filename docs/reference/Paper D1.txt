S4 Quarantine Verification: A Red-Team Harness for Namespace and LSM Isolation Claims
Abstract

We introduce a red-team benchmark harness that operationalizes quarantine claims into reproducible syscall-level tests. The harness attempts outbound networking, filesystem bypass patterns, rename-over-protected vectors, hardlink aliasing, raw sendto, AF_UNIX rendezvous, and inherited file descriptor exploitation. The suite is explicitly non-malicious and does not include exploit payloads. Results are classified as PASS, FINDING, or INFO. The harness enables regression gating in CI and ensures containment claims remain measurable.
