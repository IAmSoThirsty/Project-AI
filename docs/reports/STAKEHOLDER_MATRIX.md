## 🚨 ESCALATION MATRIX

### Incident Severity Levels

**P0 - CATASTROPHIC (CommandOverride Activated)**
- **Notify:** C-Level, Board, Ethics Board, Security Team (immediate)
- **Channel:** Emergency hotline + Slack alerts
- **Response Time:** 15 minutes
- **Review:** Post-incident review within 24 hours

**P1 - CRITICAL (Security Breach, Ethics Violation)**
- **Notify:** C-Level, Security Team, Legal (< 1 hour)
- **Channel:** Phone + Slack alerts
- **Response Time:** 1 hour
- **Review:** Post-incident review within 48 hours

**P2 - HIGH (System Outage, Data Loss)**
- **Notify:** Engineering VP, DevOps, Affected Teams (< 4 hours)
- **Channel:** PagerDuty + Slack
- **Response Time:** 4 hours
- **Review:** Post-mortem within 1 week

**P3 - MEDIUM (Performance Degradation)**
- **Notify:** Engineering Manager, Relevant Team (< 24 hours)
- **Channel:** Slack + Email
- **Response Time:** 24 hours
- **Review:** Optional post-mortem

**P4 - LOW (Minor Bug, Enhancement)**
- **Notify:** Team via ticket (< 1 week)
- **Channel:** GitHub Issues
- **Response Time:** 1 week
- **Review:** Sprint retrospective
