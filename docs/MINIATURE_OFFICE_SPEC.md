# MINIATURE OFFICE SPECIFICATION

## Overview

The **Miniature Office** is a specialized emergent microservice designed to provide a sandboxed, high-fidelity simulation of administrative and operational environments. It allows agents to perform "virtual paperwork," coordination, and organizational tasks within a contained sovereign space.

## Architecture

- **Location**: `src/app/miniature_office/`
- **Role**: Operational Support & Simulation
- **Tier**: Tier 3 (Operational Support)

## Capabilities

- **Document Management**: Simulation of filing, retrieval, and indexing.
- **Agent Lounge**: Resource-efficient coordination hub for idle agents.
- **Repair Crew**: Automated self-healing and maintenance scheduling.

## Governance

All actions within the Miniature Office are governed by the `CognitionKernel` and must adhere to the `Four Laws`.
