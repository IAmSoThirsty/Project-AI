### 4. Resilience Scoring (0-100 Scale)
**Composite Formula**:
```
Overall = (0.30 × Detection) + (0.30 × Mitigation) +

```

**9 Tracked Metrics**: Detection rate, mitigation rate, recovery speed, adaptability, detection/response times, false positive rate, component scores
